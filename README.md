### 🛒 [Customer Behavior & Statistical Analysis](https://github.com/Namitha0203/Customer_Behavior_Analysis-Statistics_Project)
 
> **Tools:** Python · Pandas · NumPy · SciPy · Matplotlib · Seaborn
 
Applied statistical analysis to a 5,000-row customer dataset to answer 10 business questions on spending, churn, and campaign effectiveness. Covered hypothesis testing, outlier detection, and the Central Limit Theorem via bootstrapping.
 
| Analysis | Method | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Gender vs Spending | Independent T-test | Male spend significantly more (p=0.0255) ✅ |
| Churn vs Product Category | Chi-Square Test | No significant link (p=0.820) |
| Regional Spending | One-Way ANOVA | No regional difference (p=0.760) |
| Campaign A vs B | Welch's T-test | No significant difference (p=0.214) |
| Outlier Detection | IQR Method | 24 high-value VIP customers identified |
 
**Descriptive stats:** Mean purchase = 1,003.95 · Median = 998.08 · Skewness = 0.108 (approx. normal)
